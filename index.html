Golf, made social

CBS Golf Academy is a student association open to everyone at Copenhagen Business School – no experience needed. Borrow clubs, meet people, and enjoy the game together.

Who we are

CBS Golf Academy is a student association at Copenhagen Business School, open to all students regardless of golf experience. Whether you have never held a club or you play every weekend, you are welcome here. Our goal is simple: to make golf accessible, social, and fun for the whole CBS community.

What we offer

Throughout the year we run a mix of training and social events – with at least one every month:

  • Indoor simulator sessions Practice on golf simulators in a relaxed setting through the winter.
  • Outdoor range sessions Work on your swing outdoors once the season starts.
  • Social rounds Casual rounds on courses around Copenhagen – play and meet new people.
  • Tournaments Friendly competitions like the CBS Open, open to every level.

No clubs? No problem – equipment is available to borrow, and our coaches are there to help beginners get started.
