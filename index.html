Golf, made social

Who we are

CBS Golf Academy brings CBS students together through a shared passion for golf. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned player, everyone is welcome. Our primary mission is to make golf accessible, enjoyable, and, above all, fun for everyone.

What we do

We host everything from casual training sessions (indoors and outdoors) to major tournaments and social events. Highlights from last year include Night Golf, Minigolf socials, and our biggest event: the CBS Open. There's something for everyone, and all CBS students are welcome.

How to find us

Stay up to date with our events and sessions by following us on social media. Our main platform is Instagram, where you'll find all our latest updates and sign-up links.

FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Still wondering something? Reach out and we'll help.

Getting started

Do I need to know how to play golf?

Not at all. Most people at our events are beginners, and our coaches are there to help you get started.

How do I become a member / join?

Sign-ups go through Orbi, and we post links on our Instagram (@golfacademycbs).

Who can join?

Any CBS student, at any level.

Does it cost anything?

You pay per event. We try to keep everything as accessible and affordable as possible.

Equipment & practical

Do I need my own clubs?

No. Clubs can be borrowed at our indoor and outdoor sessions.

What should I bring?

Just comfortable clothes. Everything else you need is provided or can be borrowed.

Events

When are events happening?

We aim for roughly one event a month. Dates are announced on our Instagram.

Contact

How do I reach you?

Email golfacademy@cbsstudents.dk or DM us on Instagram.
