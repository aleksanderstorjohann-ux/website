Join us

Anyone at CBS can join – and every level is welcome, from total beginner to seasoned player.

What to expect

  • At least one event a month Something on the calendar all year round.
  • A social, welcoming crowd Meet other students – golf is the easy part.
  • Equipment & coaching Borrow clubs and get tips from our coaches.
  • Indoor & outdoor sessions Simulators in winter, the range once the season starts.

How to join

The easiest way in is a quick message on Instagram. Say hi, tell us you'd like to join, and we'll take it from there – no forms, no fuss.
